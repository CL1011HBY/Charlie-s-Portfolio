Charlie's webpage

Charlie Lipscomb is an exceptionally talented young musician with a diverse range of skills. Having spent five years as a chorister at St John's College, Cambridge, he has honed his musical abilities to an advanced level. Charlie is currently a day pupil at Haileybury, where he is a member of Kipling House. His musical accomplishments include being diploma level in piano, Grade 7 in organ, and Grade 6 in horn, reflecting his dedication and versatility across multiple instruments. In addition to his instrumental skills, Charlie is a professional singer and was involved in the backing track for the Disney production of Shardlake. Outside of his musical achievements, Charlie is supported by his close-knit family: his sister Lydia, and his parents Charlotte and Chris, who have undoubtedly encouraged his passion for music.
